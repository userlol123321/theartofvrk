Identity

In a world that shuns the dark and gothic, The Art of Vikrant R. Kale welcomes with open arms and embraces all of that which is dark, brooding, weird and just plain gothic.

Heavily inspired by comic books, rock & metal music, theartofvrk specializes in Pen and Ink illustrations but dabbles in multidisciplinary arts. Every stroke is a testament to the beauty found in the shadows.
